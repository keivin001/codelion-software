About CODELION SOFTWARE

We bridge advanced global engineering standards with Africa's fastest-growing technology talent.

Our Purpose

Why CODELION Was Founded

CODELION was launched to deliver high-quality, enterprise-standard custom software solutions while building sustainable engineering career paths for developers locally in Rwanda.

We coordinate development tasks locally, ensuring full alignment with agile delivery cycles, code reviews, and proactive documentation. Our developers are trained in modern technologies, secure API patterns, and database scaling methodologies.

Our Mission

To design, code, and launch secure, low-latency digital systems that help global businesses scale.

Our Vision

To establish Kigali as Africa's primary center for high-end software development and DevOps management.

Quality Standards

Zero compromised files, robust automated unit test suites, and transparent version logs.

Support Guarantee

24/7 server monitoring alerts, daily reviews, and strict compliance reporting agreements.

Milestones

Our Growth Journey

A brief overview of how we scaled from a small team of three developers to Kigali's premier software engineering agency.

2021

Company Setup

Three engineers established CODELION in Kigali, focusing on building high-performance web applications and backend dashboards for local startups.

2023

Expanding to Mobile & Cloud Integration

Expanded the team to 12 engineers. Launched core products like PayLink Africa, introducing secure mobile payment aggregation models.

2025

Enterprise & AI Specializations

Established dedicated squads for cloud architecture (DevOps) and artificial intelligence/RAG databases. Partnered with 20+ global companies.
